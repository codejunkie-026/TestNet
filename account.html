Registering for Barclays Online Banking

Who can register?

To register you'll need to


  • Live in the UK
  • Be aged 16 or over
  • Have either a Barclays current account with a bank card, a savings account or a mortgage, or be a merchant customer

Once you're registered, you'll be able to see and manage all of your Barclays accounts in Online Banking, except for those that need someone else's signature.

Are you a business customer?

If you have a business savings or current account with us, please register for Business Online Banking instead.

To move to the next step or go back, use the navigation buttons at the bottom of each page. Don't use your web browser's 'back' and 'forward' buttons, as you'll lose any information you've entered.
